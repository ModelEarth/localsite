Worldwide Trade Impact And Resource Dashboard

Compare trade impact pathways and resource profiles using the same country, year, and flow controls across all four panels. Data is loaded from repository-hosted trade-data assets and aligned with the Sankey page layout.

How the partner-country score works: the score ranks import/export partner countries from best to worst on a 0 to 100 scale.

Dollar Value: score = 100 x partner dollar trade value / largest partner dollar trade value.

CO₂ Emissions and Water Use: score = 100 x (0.6 x trade strength + 0.4 x lower impact per dollar efficiency).

Employment: score = 100 x (0.6 x trade strength + 0.4 x higher jobs per dollar efficiency).

Trade strength is the partner’s dollar trade value relative to the largest partner. Efficiency compares indicator per dollar traded across the visible partner-country set.
